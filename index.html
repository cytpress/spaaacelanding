UNIQUE EXPERIENCE

EXTRAVEHICULAR ACTIVITY(EVA)

Embarking on an Extravehicular Activity (EVA) is an exhilarating venture beyond spacecraft confines. Astronauts don specialized suits, braving the void to conduct repairs, experiments, and maintenance. Meticulously planned, each EVA is a delicate ballet in microgravity, showcasing humanity's ingenuity and courage. These experiences are pivotal in advancing our understanding of space and paving the way for future exploration.

SPACE STATION TOUR

Visiting a space station offers a captivating glimpse into humanity's ventures beyond Earth. Guests experience the marvels of microgravity, witness scientific experiments in action, and learn about life in space. Guided tours showcase the station's intricate modules, advanced technologies, and daily routines of astronauts. These visits inspire curiosity and deepen understanding of space exploration's significance.

FOOTPRINTS CAPTURE

Step onto the lunar surface and capture the moment of a lifetime. Experience the awe of moonwalking as you explore the rugged terrain, feeling the crunch of regolith beneath your boots. With Earth hanging in the vast expanse above, seize the opportunity to snap breathtaking photos, immortalizing your lunar adventure forever.
